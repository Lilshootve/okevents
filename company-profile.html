About OKEVENTS

OKEVENTS is a global partner in corporate event management. We design and execute conferences, trade shows and executive events with international standards and flawless local execution. Our mission is to transform ideas into impactful experiences for businesses worldwide.

Our Services

We offer a comprehensive portfolio tailored to the needs of international corporations:

Conferences & Summits:  Agenda design, speaker management, executive hospitality and accreditation.
Trade Shows & Exhibitions:  Stand design & build, exhibitor coordination, registration and access control.
Corporate Events:  Product launches, roadshows, executive dinners and hybrid experiences.
Technical Production:  Audio, video and lighting, live streaming, interpretation and interactive screens.
Consulting & Strategy:  Event strategy, ROI framework, compliance and sustainability consultancy.
